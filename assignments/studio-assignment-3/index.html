Click on the revolving icon to test your knowledge on whether a food item is defined as a soup or a salad. Complete the quiz to see the full collection of food items.

HOW TO PLAY?



There will be an image of food item that will be shown. The question of the quiz is whether the certain food items are-

A SOUP OR A SALAD?



To answer you have to click on either the orange side / soup icon or green side / salad icon on the webpage to choose your answer. To try the quiz again just refresh the webpage.
